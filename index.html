Agent 47

About Agent 47

Agent 47 is the world's top assassin, a legendary figure in the shadowy trade of assassination.

A victim of illegal experimentation at the sinister "Institute for Human Betterment" in 1960s Romania, 47 is exceptionally intelligent with superior instincts and reflexes. A man comfortable in his own skin and in total control of his environment. An expert strategist, master of disguises and social chameleon who is always two steps ahead, he is a skilled predator: discrete, precise and endlessly inventive.

As a result of his tragic past, 47 is completely devoid of empathy, but is acutely aware of this and has "outsourced" his conscience to his long-time handler Diana Burnwood. She curates 47's contracts, targeting only the most infamous criminals as well as the wealthy, who put themselves above the law.

Together, 47 and Ms. Burnwood have developed a signature MO of disappearances, "Tragic accidents" and deaths of natural causes. Carried out with artistry, surgical precision and zero collateral damage. For this reason, 47 boasts a perfect ICA record and remains, at the very most, an urban legend.

He is a ghost: Rarely noticed and never remembered...
